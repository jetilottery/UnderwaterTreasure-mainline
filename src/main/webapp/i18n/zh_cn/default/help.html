Treasure Trawler

Are you ready to play a Match Variable game? Treasure Trawler gives you to seek to collect groups of symbols. If you collect the required number of a Collection Symbol, you win the associated prize. Also, an Instant Win prize symbol may be awarded to give you surprise!

How to Play

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

[top]

Auto Play / Stop

Press AUTO PLAY to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until all selections have been made. When AUTO PLAY button is pressed, it will be replaced by STOP button. Players can press STOP to stop automatically playing. When STOP button is pressed, it will turn back to AUTO PLAY button.

[top]

About the Game

The object of the game is to collect the required number of a Collection Symbol as well as to find the Instant Win prize symbols.

Game Screen

After a ticket is purchased, there is a treasure ship on the seabed, and there might be an octopus or a seahorse around the treasure ship. 7 opportunities are given in one game.

The player could use the steering wheel to operate the ship. If the arrow LEFT or RIGHT is pressed, the ship will go left or right respectively. If the button TRAWL is pressed, the claw will extend to somewhere of the treasure ship. During base game, there are bubbles rising from the ocean floor, which represent there is a sea animal underneath (octopus or seahorse) that might trigger Bonus Game. This design allows the player to choose the fetching location of their own.

Winning a Prize

If the player collects the required number of a Collection Symbol, the associated prize will be awarded.

If the player collects one Instant Win prize symbol, the amount denoted by the Instant Win prize symbol will be awarded.

The total win will be the sum of all winning prize values.

Game Completion

The game ends when 0 opportunity remain. A closing message appears, indicating the game’s outcome.
